Book genres you would love to read

Fantasy

Fantasy novels revolve around magic, witchcraft, supernatural, mythical creatures, etc.

Horror

Genre that seeks to provoke fear, terror, shock, and other similar kinds of feelings in readers.

Science Fiction

Science Fiction deals with the imagined future in terms of science or technology advances.

Fiction

Many of the books published today are "fiction", they contain invented material, and are creative literature.









History

Nonfiction history books include any books that lay out the known facts about a particular, time, culture, or event in history.

Poetry

Poetry is a form of verse and rhythmic writing with imagery that creates emotional responses.

Self-Help

Self-help books help people to improve a part of their life. These books usually carry topics like relationships, physical health, etc.

Comics

The stories in comic books are represented by sequential and engaging narrative art with images and dialogues.
